# Employee Onboarding Guide

## Welcome!

Welcome to the team! This guide will help you get started on your first day.

## First Day Checklist

### Before You Arrive
- [ ] Review the employee handbook
- [ ] Complete all required paperwork
- [ ] Set up your company email account

### On Your First Day
- [ ] Arrive at 9:00 AM at the main office
- [ ] Check in with reception
- [ ] Meet with your manager
- [ ] Get your employee badge and laptop
- [ ] Complete IT setup and security training

## Week One Goals

During your first week, you should:
1. Meet your team members
2. Complete all onboarding training modules
3. Set up your development environment
4. Attend the new employee orientation session
5. Schedule 1-on-1 meetings with key stakeholders

## Important Resources

- **Employee Handbook**: Available on the intranet
- **IT Support**: support@company.com or ext. 5555
- **HR Department**: hr@company.com or ext. 5000
- **Facilities**: facilities@company.com or ext. 5100

## Training Modules

All new employees must complete the following training within the first 30 days:
- Information Security Awareness
- Code of Conduct
- Diversity and Inclusion
- Workplace Safety

## Benefits Enrollment

You have 30 days from your start date to enroll in benefits:
- Health insurance
- Dental insurance
- Vision insurance
- 401(k) retirement plan
- Life insurance

Contact HR to schedule a benefits enrollment session.

## Questions?

If you have any questions during your onboarding, don't hesitate to reach out to:
- Your manager
- HR department
- Your assigned onboarding buddy

We're excited to have you on the team!
